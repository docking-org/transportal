Notes:
  • The distribution of transporters can vary across the length of the nephron. For example, PEPT1 and PEPT2 are only expressed in the proximal tubule, but both are absent from the distal tubule.
  • The arrows indicate the transporter's primary direction of transport. However, for some SLC transporters, transport in the opposite direction is also possible. For example, para-aminohippurate efflux is trans-stimulated by extracellular para-aminohippurate or glutarate in OAT1-expressing oocytes (Apiwattanakul et al., Mol Pharmacol. 1999 May;55(5):847-54).
  • The orange transporter OATP4C1 is shown, but not currently contained in our database.
